Watching the Snow

November 14, 2022

There’s something comforting about watching the snow come down, particularly when you’re warm, inside, at the window, looking out.

The sky is white, the ground goes white, even the trees trade in their black winter attire for delicate stripes of white. It gathers so slowly that you don’t see the change moment by moment, only notice it after a meditative while.
